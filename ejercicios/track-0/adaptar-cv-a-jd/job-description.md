# Job Description (oferta realista para el ejercicio)

> Esta es una oferta **ficticia pero realista** de una startup remota que paga en USD. Léela como la
> leería un ATS y un reclutador. Tu tarea (ver `README.md`) es extraer sus keywords y adaptar tu CV.

---

**Role:** AI / Automation Engineer (Remote — LATAM time zones)
**Company:** Northwind Labs · **Type:** Full-time, contractor (USD)
**Comp:** USD 3,500–5,500 / month depending on experience

### About the role

We are a small team building **agentic automations** for mid-market companies: systems that ingest
messy real-world inputs (emails, documents, tickets), use LLMs to **classify, extract, and decide**, and
then **take actions** in external systems (CRMs, ERPs, databases). You will own features end-to-end, from
spec to production, and you will be on call for the things you ship.

### What you'll do

- Design and ship **LLM-powered automations** that act on external systems with **human-in-the-loop**
  for sensitive actions.
- Build and maintain **evaluation harnesses** for our agents (we do not ship AI we cannot measure).
- Instrument everything: **traces, logs, cost and latency per request**.
- Write the **specs and ADRs**, review PRs, and keep the pipeline green.

### Must-have

- Strong **Python**. You can build a clean **FastAPI** service and test it.
- Hands-on experience with **LLM APIs** (OpenAI / Anthropic) and **function calling / tool use**.
- **RAG** fundamentals: embeddings, a vector DB (**pgvector** or similar), retrieval + reranking.
- Comfort with **PostgreSQL**, **Docker**, and **CI/CD** (GitHub Actions).
- **Professional English** (written and spoken) — the whole team works in English.
- A public **portfolio / GitHub** with projects we can actually run.

### Nice-to-have

- **LangGraph** or another agent framework.
- **Evals** tooling (ragas, DeepEval, promptfoo) and an observability stack (**Langfuse**).
- **TypeScript / Next.js** for internal tools.
- Workflow automation (**n8n**, Temporal) experience.
- Security awareness for LLM apps (**OWASP LLM Top 10**, guardrails).

### How we hire

1. We read your CV and open your GitHub. We rank for the must-haves above.
2. A 45-min technical conversation (live, spoken English, no AI assistance).
3. A take-home or a walkthrough of one of your real projects.

> If you can show one agentic automation that runs in production with evals and failure handling, you
> move to the top of the pile.
